TuneVibe

Our systems allows users to use the music website even without logging in with your account as this is some personal preference of a user. This would benefit users that like to keep their online activities private as some users may have concerns about privacy and data collection or some users may simply prefer to freely browse new songs without being tied to the recommended songs based on their music history.

However, there would be more features when logged in such as saved playlists, recommended music, search history and much more. Consequently, consumers can save more time by going to their favorite songs recommended or to their saved playlists. By subscribing, the user would also have the permit to download any song for free.

Moreover, users can also browse accordingly to categories such as genres which is different styles or genres of music, artists which is a categories dedicated to specific artists or bands, albums which is a categories that group music by specific albums and themes which is a categories that group based on moods or emotions such as "relaxing," "upbeat," or "romantic" and many more.
